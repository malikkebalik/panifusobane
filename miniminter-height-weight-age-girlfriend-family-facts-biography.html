    Miniminter Facts

  • He is a member of the YouTube collective called Ultimate Sidemen, which includes some of the most popular male YouTubers – KSI, Wroetoshaw, Behzinga, Zerkaa, Vikkstar123, and TBJZL.
  • He first met his Ultimate Sidemen groupmate KSI, when they were studying in secondary school. They absolutely hated each other and even got into a physical fight at the age of 12 or 13.
  • As he couldn’t get sufficiently good grades in GCSE exams, he couldn’t get into his preferred college in 2011. Therefore, he decided to take a year off. During his year off, he lived in Ghana for 2 months, where he taught soccer to the locals. Then, in the summer, he traveled to the United States, where he lived in 6 states on the east coast.
  • He was inspired by KSI to create his own YouTube channel. Also, after dropping out of the university, he briefly worked on KSI’s clothing line, Beast.
  • He created his YouTube account in February 2008 but didn’t seriously consider posting regular content for almost 4 years.
  • In December 2012, he uploaded his first FIFA video. He had created it just after having left the university.
  • In June 2016, he served as a captain of the Ultimate Sidemen team in a charity soccer match against YouTube All-Stars.
