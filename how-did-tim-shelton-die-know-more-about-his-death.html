How Did Tim Shelton Die? Know More About His Death

August 2024 · 2 minute read

In a heartbreaking incident during Saturday’s Iron Man Race at Shelby Farms, Tim Shelton, a cherished pastor from Bellevue Baptist Church, suffered a cardiac arrest while swimming. Tragically, he passed away on Sunday, October 8, 2023, at Baptist East. Pastor Tim was deeply loved within the community, leaving behind a void that will be profoundly felt by all.

Who Was Tim Shelton?

Tim Shelton, a native of Johnson City, Tennessee, later relocated to Memphis, Tennessee, where he dedicated his efforts to Bellevue Baptist Church. He pursued his education at Mid-America Baptist Theological Seminary and the University of Tennessee. Tim was a proud graduate of Science Hill High School. In his personal life, he found immense joy in his marriage to Susan Stephens Shelton, and together, they raised five boys. Tim was widely recognized for his unwavering kindness and compassion. Renowned as one of the most diligent workers, he poured his heart and soul into everything he did, leaving a lasting impact on everyone he encountered.

How Did Tim Shelton Die?

According to reports, he suffered a cardiac arrest while participating in the Iron Man race at Shelby Farms on Saturday, October 7th. Tim was swiftly taken to the hospital, where he was put on a ventilator. Unfortunately, Tim passed away on Sunday morning. The tragic incident unfolded while he was in the water, a close source revealed, and it took a harrowing 30 minutes before medical responders were able to restore his heartbeat.

Following the devastating news of Tim Shelton’s passing, an outpouring of condolences and support has come forth for his family. BPC Performance Coaching shared a heartfelt post on their Facebook page to express their sympathy and support during this difficult time.
