Did Violet Myers Pass Away?

September 2024 · 4 minute read

Violet Myers is not dead. She was rumored to have passed away in November 2021 due to an unknown cause. 

The rumor of her death caused quite a huge buzz on the internet as she is well known as an adult film actress, director, model, and Instagram influencer. She is popular for posting sizzling pictures on her social media handles and also for her appearances in various movies. In this article, you will get to know all about the actress’s rise to fame, her career, and rumors surrounding her death.

Who is Violet Myers?

Violet Myers is an actress, director, model, and Instagram influencer well known by her nickname, Luna Bunny. She was born on February 24, 1997, to a Mexican mother and a Turkish father. The actress is private when it comes to details of her family, and as such, the names of her family members and what they are up to are unknown.

The names of the schools that she attended have been scarce, but it was revealed that she graduated from a local high school. There is also no information about whether or not she earned a degree.

What Happened To The Actress?

On November 2021, the news of the death of Violet Myers began to make headlines on the internet. At the time of the rumor, the actress was at the peak of her career. The cause of her death and how she died were not made known to the public. Nevertheless, many online sources later reported that she died of a heart attack.

Following the news, condolence messages from her fans and friends began to hit the internet. However, the information was not confirmed by her family or any authoritative source. It eventually turned out that the news of the adult actress’ death was just a rumor. This has been confirmed by her uploads on her social media handles and other sources.
