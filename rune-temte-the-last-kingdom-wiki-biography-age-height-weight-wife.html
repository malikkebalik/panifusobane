Rune Temte Biography

Temte gave up his football career to pursue acting after five years with Strømsgodset Football Club. In 1993–1994, Temte trained at the Drama Studio London. He continued to play football while training for Tooting and Mitcham United F.C.

Temte was also a member of the bandy team Solberg SK, where he played for over four decades. He was a midfielder for the Norwegian Y-19 squad at the 1984 Bandy World Championship in Vänersborg, Sweden, when they won the bronze medal.

Rune Temte Acting career

Temte founded his own production company, Temte Productions, in 1991 and has since produced more than 20 theatre shows, one full-length film, and three short films. He has also performed on the stage.
