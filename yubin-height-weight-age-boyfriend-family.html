Yubin is a South Korean singer, songwriter, rapper, musician, businessperson, actor, and television personality who is the founder and CEO of the entertainment agency RRR Entertainment. Yubin gained popularity as a member of the South Korean girl group Wonder Girls which was formed in 2007 by JYP Entertainment. The group’s discography consists of albums, extended plays, and tracks like The Wonder Years, Wonder World, Reboot, The Wonder Begins, Why So Lonely, 2 Different Tears, Nobody for Everybody, Wonder Party, It’s Not Love (미안한 마음), Irony, Tell Me, Nobody, This Fool (이 바보), Be My Baby, I Feel You, and Why So Lonely. Wonder Girls disbanded in 2017 and Yubin made her debut as a solo artist in 2018. As a solo artist, she has released songs such as Lady (숙녀 (淑女)), Silent Movie (무성영화) (ft. Yoon Mi-rae), Wave, Thank U Soooo Much, Perfume (향수), and Yaya (Me Time) (넵넵 (ME TIME)). Additionally, Yubin has also been a part of various shows and movies such as King of Mask Singer, The Last Godfather, The Virus, H Club, Lovely Voice: The Beginning, We’re Family, Things These Days, The Wonder Girls, The Last Survivor, Chocolate, VIP, Queen of Ssireum, Unpretty Rapstar 2, Goal Girls, Dating is Straight, The Second World, and Here He Comes.

    Yubin Facts

  • She was a tomboy during her younger years and sported short hair.
  • Along with G.NA, After School‘s Uee, Secret‘s Jun Hyo-seong, and Spica‘s Yang Ji-won, Yubin was originally set to debut as a member of Five Girls under Good Entertainment but the band disbanded before its scheduled debut in 2007 due to Good Entertainment’s financial struggles. The members subsequently joined different South Korean entertainment companies.

  • In 2019, she appeared as Cha Se-rin in the mystery drama television series VIP.
  • She played the role of Oh Jeong-bok in the television drama series Chocolate in 2020.
