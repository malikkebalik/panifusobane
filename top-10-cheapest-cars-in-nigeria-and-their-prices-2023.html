Top 10 Cheapest Cars In Nigeria And Their Prices 2023

August 2024 · 6 minute read

The Top 10 Cheapest Cars in Nigeria 2023 offer affordable options for car buyers. Whether you’re looking for a new or used vehicle, these choices come with budget-friendly prices. With a focus on simplicity and accessibility, these cars ensure that becoming a car owner doesn’t break the bank.

From compact models to practical options, there’s something for everyone. So, let’s dive in and explore the affordable cars available in Nigeria.

1. Toyota Camry

Introducing the Toyota Camry, one of the most affordable cars in Nigeria for 2023. Known for its popularity, easy maintenance, and fuel economy, the Camry has earned a place on the streets of Nigeria.

With its comfortable interior and built-in safety features, brakes, tires, and engines, it offers a reliable driving experience.

Toyota caters to both the affluent and average-income earners, providing a range of Camry models at varying prices. While brand new Camry models may cost around ₦3,500,000, used options such as the 2008 model can be found for approximately ₦1,700,000.

It stands out as one of the most affordable cars in Nigeria for 2023.

2. Toyota Corolla

The Toyota Corolla is a well-known and inexpensive car in Nigeria. It dominates the roads here and in many other countries. With various models available, the prices differ accordingly. it is a top-selling car globally and remains at the forefront of the automobile industry.

This brand is recognized for its affordability, dependability, and durability. The Corolla is also praised for its fuel efficiency, easy access to spare parts, decent comfort, and low maintenance costs.

A used Corolla can be purchased for around ₦900,000 to ₦3,500,000, while a new model, like the 2020 version, may cost approximately ₦6,000,000, depending on the seller and vehicle condition.

3. Volkswagen Beetle

The Volkswagen Beetle is a compact two-door car. It has earned various local names in different countries, including Nigeria, where it has had a presence. While it may not be as prevalent on Nigerian roads, the Beetle offers affordability and a charming appeal.

In terms of fuel efficiency and maintenance costs, the Beetle is considered economical. It is also known for its strength and durability. One can acquire a Volkswagen Beetle for around ₦500,000 or a similar price range.

Overall, the Volkswagen Beetle presents an affordable and enduring choice, making it an attractive option for budget-conscious car buyers in Nigeria.

4. Honda CR-V

The Honda CR-V is a popular and economical car commonly found on Nigerian roads. Made by the Japanese automaker, it showcases a sleek and sturdy design, with each new model surpassing the previous one.

Honda is renowned for creating durable and trustworthy vehicles, and the CR-V exemplifies these qualities.

In Nigeria, prices for used models range from approximately ₦800,000 to ₦2.1 million, depending on factors such as the specific model and the seller.

5. Toyota Sienna

The Toyota Sienna is a standout option and is widely known for being one of the most affordable choices available. Families, in particular, love the Sienna for its spacious interior and versatility.

In addition to its affordability, the Sienna is also recognized for its fuel efficiency, easy handling, and overall comfort.

If you’re in Nigeria, you can easily find used Toyota Sienna models from 2001 to 2011, with prices ranging from ₦900,000 to ₦5,600,000. The price may vary based on factors such as the condition of the vehicle, mileage, and manufacturing year.

6. Volkswagen Golf

When it comes to budget-friendly cars in Nigeria, the Volkswagen Golf stands out. It is renowned for its affordability and sleek design. The Golf offers a comfortable driving experience and excellent fuel efficiency.

In 2023, prices for the Volkswagen Golf range from ₦550,000 to ₦900,000 for earlier models. Newer models can be found starting from ₦2,500,000. Whether you’re looking for a stylish upgrade or a cost-effective option, the Volkswagen Golf is a top choice.

7. Kia Soul

When it comes to affordable cars in Nigeria, the Kia Soul stands out as a top choice. It offers a distinctive design and excellent value for money. The Soul provides a comfortable and enjoyable driving experience, along with good fuel efficiency.

In 2023, prices for the Kia Soul start from as low as ₦600,000. With its affordable price tag and unique style, the Kia Soul is a popular and budget-friendly option for car buyers in Nigeria.

8. Honda Accord

When it comes to affordable cars in Nigeria, the Honda Accord is a top contender. Known for its reliability and value for money, the Accord is a popular option for many car buyers.

In 2023, you can find used Honda Accord models starting from ₦1,800,000, with prices varying based on factors such as condition and mileage.

With its fuel efficiency, low maintenance costs, and smooth handling, the Honda Accord offers an economical and dependable driving experience.

9. Mercedes Benz C200

Even amidst luxury cars, the Mercedes Benz C200 stands out as an affordable option in Nigeria. Known for its elegance and sophistication, the C200 offers a taste of luxury without breaking the bank.

In 2023, you can find used Mercedes Benz C200 models starting from ₦1,000,000 with prices varying depending on factors such as year, mileage, and condition.

With its sleek design, advanced features, and prestigious brand reputation, the C200 delivers both style and value for money.

10. Lexus RX300

When it comes to affordable cars in Nigeria, the Lexus RX300 is a standout option. This SUV offers a perfect blend of luxury and affordability.

With its spacious interior and sleek design, the RX300 is sure to turn heads. In 2023, you can find the Lexus RX300 at a price starting from ₦1,000,000, making it an excellent choice for budget-conscious buyers.

Experience the comfort and reliability of the RX300 without breaking the bank.

Here is a summary list of the Top 10 Cheapest Cars In Nigeria And Their Prices in 2023:

1. Toyota Camry

2. Toyota Corolla

3. Volkswagen Beetle

4. Honda CR-V

5. Toyota Sienna

6. Volkswagen Golf

7. Kia Soul

8. Honda Accord

9. Mercedes Benz C200

10. Lexus RX300

Conclusion

In conclusion, the Top 10 Cheapest Cars in Nigeria 2023 offer affordable choices for prospective car owners. With reasonable prices and various options, these vehicles provide accessible solutions for different needs and budgets.

Make your car ownership dreams a reality without breaking the bank.
