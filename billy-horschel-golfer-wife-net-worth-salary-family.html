Billy Horschel golfer career and news

Horschel completed third at the True South Classic 2012 but only ended 147th on the money list in 2012. After his third attempt in four trips to Q School, he received his PGA Tour Card in 2013.

He held the joint lead with Phil Mickelson, at the halfway stage, in the U.S. Open 2013. After, Horschel earned his 2nd victory at the BMW Championship 2014 and moved to the 23rd round in the world. He won the Zurich Classic of New Orleans with Scott Piercy in April 2018.

Horschel won the WGC-Dell Technologies Match Play, overcoming Scottie Scheffler 2 and 1 in the final in March 2021.

Horschel was chosen to represent the U.S. team in the 2022 Presidents Cup held in September. Out of the three matches he participated in, he emerged victorious in one and suffered defeat in two.
