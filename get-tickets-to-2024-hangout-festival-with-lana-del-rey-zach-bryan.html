It may be cold out now but we’re already in a spring state of mind.

That’s because Gulf Shores, AL’s annual beach-based Hangout Festival just announced their sunny, star-studded lineup.

This year’s edition, set to take place at Gulf Shores Beach from May 17-19, features big name headliners like legendary songstress Lana Del Rey, country icon Zach Bryan, party starters Odesza, “Euphoria” star and singer-songwriter Dominic Fike and “Mean Girls'” own Regina George herself, Reneé Rapp.

They’ll be joined by The Chainsmokers, Cage The Elephant, Subtronics, Jessie Murph and Sexxy Red.

Plus, old school Hip-Hop hero Nelly will be there, too.

All in all, 61 (!) huge acts are set to rock out at the beach over the long mid-May weekend.
