Faith Jenkins And Kenny Lattimore Are Expecting Their First Child

July 2024 · 3 minute read

On August 11, Faith Jenkins announced to her followers that she was expecting her first child. The American lawyer and her husband, Kenny Lattimore, have stated that they are “expecting our greatest blessing yet.” She posted an adorable photo of herself, Kenny Lattimore, and their dog, who is holding a sweet poster that reads:

“My parents are going to get me a human.”

Jenkins expressed her joy in the post that they would soon be holding a child. She mentioned the following:

“I couldn’t have asked for a better husband, life partner, and – now – father to our soon-to-be little human, Kenny, as I’ve said countless times over the last three years!! We can’t express how excited we are for the next chapter of our lives!”

Kenny Lattimore, Faith Jenkins’ husband

Faith Jenkins, 44, is an American attorney and legal analyst best known for her work on MSNBC. She also worked as a television arbitrator on the Judge Faith TV Show, which was canceled in 2018. She is now a presiding judge and the long-running host of the courtroom drama Divorce Court.

The conflict between separated and divorced couples is the focus of the television show. She is set to leave the show in September 2022, to be replaced by Star Jones., She also co-hosts the crime show Killer Relationship with Faith Jenkins, which looks into tragic romantic relationships. Jenkins is currently working on the second season of the show.

In her personal life, she is married to singer-songwriter Kenny Lattimore, whom she married in March 2020 at the First Congregational Church in Los Angeles. They exchanged photos from their special day as well as heartfelt messages. Faith Jenkins attended the wedding in a laced-up strapless A-line gown with a long sheer veil. Lattimore looked dapper in a tuxedo with two vests and gold buttons.

The lawyer wrote on Instagram:

“Today was a total nightmare!” Stevie Wonder sang one of my favorite songs as I walked down the aisle to marry my true love—a man who continues to amaze me with his love, honor, and leadership every day of our journey.”

For their second wedding anniversary in March of this year, Kenny wrote a long note to his wife and included a throwback photo from their wedding day. According to Kenny:

“I’ve never taken a new beginning, like the dawn of a new day, for granted. Our anniversary is only one day long, but our love and bond will live on forever! As the saying goes, every love story is unique, but ours will always be my favorite. “Happy Anniversary, my beautiful bride and love!”

Previously, the couple had expressed an interest in pre-marital counseling. On Instagram in 2019, Faith Jenkins thanked their wedding officiant DeVon Franklin for being one of their advisers. Faith Jenkins discussed her relationship with Kenny Lattimore with ESSENCE in December 2020. She reminisced about a “nice conversation with a nice person,” saying:

“I didn’t walk away from our first lunch date thinking, ‘That’s my husband…'” I left with the impression that it had been a pleasant conversation with a pleasant individual. I gradually realized how wonderful a person he is and how he is everything I desired.”

Kenny and his ex-wife, singer Chanté Moore, have a 19-year-old son named Kenny Lattimore Jr.
