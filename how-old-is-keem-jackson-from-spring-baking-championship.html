Spring Baking Championship is an American food competition television show that airs on Food Network. Every episode of the series format has two rounds. The first round, known as the “PreHeat,” requires the participants to make a baked treat while keeping a certain theme in mind. In the next match, the first-round winner enjoys an advantage.

The “Main Heat” is the second phase, when contestants create a larger confection that typically keeps to the pre-heat concept. The bakers are required to add a twist to the final product before it is revealed in the middle of the central heat. The winner advances to the next episode, while the baker who created the weakest dish in the “Main Heat” is eliminated. The three surviving bakers will compete in the last “Main Heat” challenge.
