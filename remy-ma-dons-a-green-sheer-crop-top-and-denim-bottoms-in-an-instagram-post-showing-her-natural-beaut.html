Remy Ma Dons a Green Sheer Crop Top and Denim Bottoms in an Instagram Post Showing Her Natural Beaut

Acclaimed rapper and reality star Remy Ma looked fabulous in a sheer green crop top and stylish denim bottoms. See the hip-hop star's stunning snaps.

"Love & Hip Hop" star Remy Ma continues to show off her timeless beauty. She recently posted two photos to her Instagram account that showcased her undeniable beauty.

The gorgeous pictures showed Remy posing for the camera while wearing a long-sleeved green sheer crop top and denim bottoms. Her voguish outfit revealed her black bra and her ample bosom.

Remy showed off her long wavy curls that cascaded down her shoulders and chest. The "Melanin Magic" singer finished her look with silver heart-shaped earrings and a necklace.

The necklace had a pendant bearing her name. Remy's fans and followers raved about her whole ensemble, especially her makeup-free look. One admirer commented:

Like the Grammy-nominated rapper's timeless beauty, her singing career continues to flourish for over a decade. Back in the '90s, Remy was a part of the hip-hop group Terror Squad.

She became one of the most influential rappers at that time. After debuting her 2006 album, Remy released more songs such as "Whateva" and "Conceited."

But her career took a turn for the worse after she allegedly shot a member of her entourage in 2007. The following year, she was convicted of intentional assault and sentenced to eight years in prison.

Remy was released from jail in 2014 and was able to reunite with her husband, rapper Papoose. Following the unfortunate incident that ruined her reputation, she is determined to restore it.

She recently shared her story on TV One's "Uncensored" series. The "Money Showers" hitmaker wanted to show people a different side of herself amid her negative reputation.

Remy's upbringing in Bronx, New York, had contributed to her rough personality. She endured misfortunes at a young age which involved drug raids and jail time while taking care of her young siblings.

Even though she has faced hardships throughout her life, she sometimes wonders how she was able to survive. She is thankful to have regained her sanity and enjoy a peaceful life with her family.

Remy and Papoose, who had suffered a painful miscarriage in the past, welcomed their adorable daughter Reminisce Mackenzie in December 2018 through in vitro fertilization or IVF.

They nicknamed their two-year-old daughter the "golden child." The couple, who has been married for 13 years, has planned to have a second child through an IVF procedure.
