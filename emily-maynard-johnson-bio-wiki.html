What is the relationship of Emily Maynard Johnson?

Emily Maynard Johnson is currently married to Tyler Johnson. They met each other at Forest Hills Church in Charlotte. Their first date was in Jan 2013.

They exchanged vows on June 7, 2014, on the groom’s family farm in Sharon, South Carolina. It was a rustic glam wedding under the 150 years old oak tree and was attended by 200 guests.

The wedding’s maid of honor was none other than Emily’s 8 years old daughter, Ricki. The bride wore a custom strapless pink tulle Modern Trousseau dress. The wedding cake was four-tiered and the return gift for guests was burlap bags filled with local goodies.

Then on July 16, 2015, Emily gave birth to Tyler’s son, Jennings. Their second son, Gibson was born on Sep 16, 2016, and the third son, Gatlin was born on Nov 12, 2017.

Their fourth child was born in Oct 2020. The name of their daughter is yet not disclosed.

Previous Relationship

Emily was in a relationship with a NASCAR driver, Ricky Hendrick from 1999. They exchanged rings on Oct 24, 2004. But after six months, Ricky died in a plane crash.

From this relationship, Emily has a daughter, Ricki Hendrick (Josephine Riddick Hendrick). She was born on June 29, 2005.

In March 2011, Emily won The Bachelor S15 and became the fiancee of Brad Womack, an entrepreneur. But they called off their relationship in June 2011.

After this, Emily engaged with Jef holm of The Bachelorette S8 in July 2012. But later in the same year, they broke up.

Note: Her husband Tyler Johnson is not an NBA player.

Who is Emily Maynard Johnson?

Emily Maynard Johnson is an American TV personality and author. She is known as a contestant on the reality shows The Bachelor S15 and The Bachelorette S8.

She also writes blogs on her self-named online website.

Emily Maynard Johnson- Age, Patents, Ethnicity, Education

Emily was born on Feb 1, 1986, in Morgantown, West Virginia, USA. Her ethnicity is Caucasian.

She went to a boarding school in Florida. Details about her father and mother are not yet disclosed.

Emily Maynard Johnson- Professional Life

Emily Maynard Johnson is an event planner by profession. She has worked as a volunteer at the Levine Children’s hospital and the Hendrick Marrow Program. She runs a fashion, beauty, and lifestyle blog.

The Bachelor (Season 15)

The Bachelor is a dating reality show and its season 15 was aired from Jan 13, 2011, to March 14, 2011.

Emily appeared as a contestant in this show along with 28 contestants to win the heart of Brad Womack. He is an entrepreneur and bar owner.

Finally, Emily won the season and became Womack’s fiancee. But they broke up on June 29, 2011.

The Bachelorette (Season 8)

After The Bachelor, Emily was seen in another dating game show, The Bachelorette S8. In it, there were 25 male participants to win a heart of Emily. It was aired from May 2012 till July 2012.

Emily Maynard Johnson accepted the proposal of Jef Holm who is an entrepreneur. They got engaged in 2012 but called off the engagement in Oct 2012.
