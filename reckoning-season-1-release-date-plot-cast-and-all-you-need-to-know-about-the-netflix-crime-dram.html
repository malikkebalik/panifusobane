'Reckoning' Season 1: Release date, plot, cast and all you need to know about the Netflix crime dram

August 2024 · 2 minute read

If you're into shows that involve dubious serial killers posing as part of the community, 'Reckoning' might be just the series for you. The series was initially released in October 2019 in New Zealand and will debut in the United States in May 2020. Follow the journey of a hidden killer as a cop obsessed with finding him turns the case of the dead teenager into thrilling havoc.

Release date

'Reckoning' will premiere on May 1, 2020.

Plot

We explore the mind's darkest corners through the perspective of two fathers, homicide detective Mike Serrato (Aden Young) and high-school counselor Leo Doyle (Sam Trammell) — one of whom is a serial killer. As the men try to do what is best for their families in order to protect them, they have to battle their own inner demons. In the midst of their personal darkness, a serial killer who hasn't killed in years shows face as the murder of teenager rattles the cages of their suburban community.

Cast

Milly Alcock

Milly Alcock is an Australian actress known for her role as Cindi Jackson in 'Janet King' (2017), Emma Carvolth in 'A Place to call Home' (2018), Marissa Campbell in 'Pine Gap' (2018), Maya Nordenfelt in 'Fighting Season' (2018) and Jenny McGinty in 'The Gloaming' (2020)

Paula Arundell as Sheriff Waller

Paula Arundell is an actress, known for 'Gods of Egypt' (2016), as Talikaa in 'Farscape' (1999) and as Nurse Jean in 'Harmony' (2018)

Other members of the cast include Sean Barker as Edgar Harris, Nic English as Brad, Gloria Garayua, Diana Glenn, Laura Gordon, Simone Kessell, Finn Little as Jake Serrato, Di Smith as Dotty, Sana’a Shaik as Lacey Diaz and John Batchelor as Detective Purcell

Creators

The series is directed by Shawn Seet, Jennifer Leacey, and Peter Andrikidis. The series writing credits go to Katrina Cabrera Ortega, David Hubbard, Ryan Mottesheard and Thompson Evans. Diane Haddon and Ryan Mottesheard serve as producers.

Trailer

The trailer is yet to be released, stay tuned for updates.

Where to watch

Catch Season 1 of 'Reckoning' on May 1, 2020, on Netflix.
