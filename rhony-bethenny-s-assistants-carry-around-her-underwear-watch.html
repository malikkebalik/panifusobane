RHONY: Bethennys Assistants Carry Around Her Underwear Watch

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Have you ever wondered what it might be like to work for Bethenny Frankel? In this EXCLUSIVE peak at the life of the 'Real Housewives of New York' super mogul, we get to see first hand from her assistants what it means to be Team Bethenny. Bethenny Frankel, 44, is back on The Real Housewives of New York this season, and that means she's busier than ever. Of course, the Skinny Girl founder isn't in it alone. In an EXCLUSIVE clip, we get to hear from Beth's personal assistant and stylist, also known as the women who help make Bethenny's entire empire run smoothly-- or, as smoothly as possible given the insane circumstances of her life. Check out the special video now!


Have you ever wanted to work for one of the Real Housewives? Maybe, maybe not, but after watching this clip, you’re guaranteed to feel impressed by the people who know Ms. Frankel as boss.

“No two days are the same,” Meghan Herd, Bethenny’s executive assistant, explains. Her stylist, Julia Kitziger, echoes those feelings. “I need to be alert and know her schedule as it’s changing,” she says. “What she needs when she needs it is there at all times.”

We then get an example of what exactly Julia meant by that! Ushering Bethenny into the bathroom to change, she tells the two women, “I don’t have panties– tell me you brought…” Bethenny says. “Yup,” Julia responds. See?

Julia also explains what Bethenny is like as a person. “Whatever you’re doing [for her], you have to do it like it is the only job you have ever done,” she says. “She’s supportive, but she teaches a lot… There are times to be professional and there are times to kick your feet up and have fun,” she adds.

Would you ever want to work for Bethenny? Tune in to The Real Housewives of New York City on Tuesdays at 9pm ET/PT on Bravo.
