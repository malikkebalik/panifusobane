Honey Boo Boo Star Anna Cardwell, 29, Succumbs to Cancer After a Brave Battle

July 2024 · 3 minute read

Reality TV personality Anna Cardwell, known for her appearance on TLC’s “Here Comes Honey Boo Boo,” has tragically passed away at the age of 29.

The sad news was shared by her family on social media, revealing that Cardwell lost her battle with cancer on Saturday night.

On Sunday, Alana Thompson (Honey Boo Boo) and June Shannon (Mama June), Cardwell’s sister and mother, respectively, took to Instagram to announce the devastating news.

June Shannon shared that Anna Cardwell had fought courageously against cancer for ten months but, unfortunately, succumbed to the disease.

“She gave one hell of a fight for 10 months,” expressed Shannon, reflecting on Cardwell’s brave struggle with the illness. The family shared their deep grief and appreciation for the support they had received during this challenging time.

Anna Cardwell passed away at 11:12 p.m. on Saturday at her mother’s home in Milledgeville, Georgia.

The statement from the family’s manager and close friend, Gina Rodriguez, conveyed that Cardwell was surrounded by her daughters, sisters, husband, and other close family members in her final moments.

The family requested prayers during this difficult time and mentioned the poignant detail that Anna passed away on the birthday of her youngest daughter, Kylee.

In the heartfelt posts via social media, relatives sympathized with their distress over Cardwell’s passing. Alana Thompson, sincerely crushed, referenced that Anna Cardwell had been in extreme pain yet found a sense of peace in her last hours.

Thompson expressed her heartbreak, portraying the trouble of watching her sister fight the disease throughout the last year.

“I really don’t know what to say, as my heart is completely broken,” composed Thompson. She underscored Cardwell’s versatility, alluding to her as a contender.

Anna Cardwell’s Battle Against Cancer

While Anna Cardwell didn’t make a formal declaration about her cancer diagnosis, she shared her excursion and difficulties on Instagram and TikTok over time.

In July, June Shannon revealed to Entertainment Tonight that Cardwell was managing an aggressive stage 4 disease, depicting what was going on as terminal.

In November, Cardwell updated her followers on TikTok, expressing that she was doing “pretty good” subsequent to going through radiation therapy and immune system treatment.

In spite of the terminal nature of her sickness, Cardwell kept an uplifting perspective and kept updating her allies on her wellbeing.

Anna Cardwell acquired public attention through her appearances on “Here Comes Honey Boo Boo,” a reality TV drama that followed the existences of the Thompson family.

The show, which circulated from 2012 to 2014, was a spin-off of “Toddlers and Tiaras” and displayed the family’s everyday encounters.

Following the wiping out of “Here Comes Honey Boo Boo” in 2014, members of the Thompson family kept on navigating reality television’s open doors. June Shannon, specifically, sought after projects on WE TV.

At the end of the social media announcement, the family shared their lasting love for Anna Cardwell.

Alana Thompson’s strong words, “We will always love you, Anna,” epitomized the profound feeling of misfortune felt by the family and the enduring effect of Cardwell’s presence in their lives.
