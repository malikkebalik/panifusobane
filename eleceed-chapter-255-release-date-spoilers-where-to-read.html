Eleceed Chapter 255: Release Date, Spoilers &amp; Where to Read

August 2024 · 4 minute read

Fans of the action-packed manhwa Eleceed have been eagerly awaiting the release of Chapter 255. In this chapter, Jinwoo continues his training to become even stronger, with Schnauder, Roist, and Suprei keeping a close eye on his progress. The story takes an exciting turn as Jinwoo gains the recognition he has long desired. Additionally, readers may anticipate the appearance of Kayden in the upcoming chapter. In this article, we will provide the release date of Eleceed Chapter 255 and guide you on where to read it.

Recap of Chapter 254

Chapter 254 of Eleceed begins with Duke Grane reflecting on his fight with Seo Jin-woo. The consequences of that day’s events weigh heavily on Duke, as Roist and Schnauzer show no signs of letting it slide. Amidst his worries, Elay Pigarun pays him a visit.

Elay Pigarun confronts Duke, acknowledging his defeat despite his attempt to cheat. He mentions that without Roist’s intervention, Schnauder, who initially showed little interest in taking a disciple, would not have taken Duke under his wing. Then he advises Duke to inform Schnauzer that he sustained injuries during training, which hindered his ability to fight Seo Jiwoo effectively. Duke is perplexed by Elay’s assistance, to which Elay reveals that their shared goal is to eliminate Seo Jiwoo.

Elay Pigarun’s immense strength makes him a formidable opponent, and Duke considers joining forces with him. Elay discloses that Schnauzer has visited Kayden’s apprentice, shocking Duke as he realizes his master’s visit excluded him.

Later, Schnauzer meets Seo Jin-woo and questions him about his victory over Duke, despite the cheating involved. While Schnauzer acknowledges Jinwoo’s prowess, he asserts that Jinwoo is still weak. Schnauzer then inquires about Kayden’s whereabouts, and Roist informs him that Seo Jiwoo arrived alone from the beginning.

Suprei also pays a visit to Jinwoo, questioning his presence despite his disciple’s defeat. Schnauzer explains that Duke was merely a source of entertainment and that he took him as a disciple only five years ago, making him incomparable to Kayden’s apprentice. Jinwoo corrects Schnauzer, stating that he has been Kayden’s apprentice for about a year, leaving everyone in the room astonished.

Preview and Spoilers of Eleceed Chapter 255

In Chapter 255, readers can anticipate Jinwoo’s continued journey to become stronger. With his newfound recognition, he must live up to his reputation and face the challenges ahead. The potential introduction of Kayden in this chapter adds an exciting dynamic to the story. As the plot progresses, the fate of the characters hangs in the balance, leaving readers eager for the next installment.
